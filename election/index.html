For Members

Here are the two simple steps that you should do to vote:

  1. Enroll yourself in the voters list by registering yourself in this website before 14-April-2014.
  2. Cast your vote on 15-April-2014 between 11 AM and 11 PM by clicking on the Vote button

Please register yourself within the electoral roll before 14-April-2014 to be eligible for voting

For Candidates

If you wish to be a candidate:

  • Ensure that you have enrolled in voters list by registering in this website.
  • Now click on "Submit Nomination" button to submit your nomination form before 12-April-2014 to be eligible for contesting in elections.
  • Click on the Rules and Regulations and Call for Nomination buttons for more details on the entire process

The link for voting will be enabled on 15-Apr-2014
