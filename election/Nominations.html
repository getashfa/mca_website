Procedure for filing Nomination forms

Here are the three simple steps that you should do to file your nomination:

1 Download the nomination form attached herewith Nomination Form
2. Fill the nomination form
3 Send the completed nomination form along with a passport size photograph to iitbmca.elections@gmail.com from your gpo id





IMPORTANT NOTE

The proposer and seconder also have to send mails to iitbmca.elections@gmail.com from their respective gpo id's





Do read the Election Rules before filling up the forms
